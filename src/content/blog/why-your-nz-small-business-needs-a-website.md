If you run a small business in New Zealand, chances are someone has searched for you online this week — whether they found you or not.

## First impressions happen before the phone call

Most customers check a business out online before ever picking up the phone. A clear, fast, mobile-friendly site tells them you're legitimate, active, and easy to work with.

## It works while you don't

A website answers the questions you'd otherwise field one at a time — pricing, service areas, hours, how to get in touch — any time of day, without you lifting a finger.

## Good SEO compounds

Every page you publish is another chance to show up in local search results. Over time, that adds up to steady, low-cost leads instead of one-off ad spend.
