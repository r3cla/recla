Privacy Notice

Information Collection

I may collect personal information from you, including:

Purpose of Collection

I collect your personal information in order to:

• Provide web development services and discuss project needs
• Communicate about project requirements and progress
• Process payments and send invoices
• Meet legal and regulatory requirements
• Maintain necessary business records
• Improve my services and user experience

Optional Information

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter certain details, I may be unable to provide adequate service. I will clearly indicate which information is mandatory for service provision.

Data Storage and Security

I keep your information safe by storing it in a secure, encrypted environment with access limited to myself (the developer). I maintain regular security updates and monitoring, along with secure backup procedures. Client data is retained for as long as needed for business purposes or as required by law, typically for 7 years for financial records.

Information Sharing

I do not sell your personal information to third parties. I may only share your information with:

• Service providers necessary for website functionality (e.g., hosting services, payment processors)
• Legal authorities when required by law
• Professional advisors (e.g., accountants, lawyers) when necessary

Any third parties who receive your data are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

Cookies and Tracking

I may use cookies, analytics, or similar technologies on websites I develop to enhance the user experience and collect anonymous usage data. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit certain website functionalities.

Your Rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

• Access your personal information
• Request corrections if you believe information is incorrect
• Request deletion of your data (where applicable)
• Make a complaint to the Privacy Commissioner

If you are an EU resident, additional GDPR rights may apply to your data processing, including the right to data portability and the right to restrict processing.

Policy Updates

I may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in services or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated to active clients via email.

If you'd like to request a copy of your information, have it corrected, or have any privacy-related questions, please contact me at kiaora@logie.nz.
